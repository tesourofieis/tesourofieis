[Rule]

[Oratio]
O God, in defense of Whose honor Stanislaus, the glorious Bishop, died by the~
swords of wicked men, grant, we beseech You, that all who seek his help may~
obtain salvation as a result of his prayers.
$Per Dominum

[Secreta]
Sanctify, O Lord, the gifts dedicated to You, and by the intercession of Blessed~
Stanislaus, Your Martyr and Bishop, let them move You to look favorably upon us.
$Per Dominum

[Postcommunio]
May this Communion, O Lord, cleanse us from guilt, and, through the intercession~
of Blessed Stanislaus, Your Martyr and Bishop, make us share in the heavenly remedy.
$Per Dominum
