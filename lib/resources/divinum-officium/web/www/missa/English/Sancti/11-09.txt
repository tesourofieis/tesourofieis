[Secreta]
Look with favor upon our prayers, we beseech You, O Lord, that as we offer our~
prayers before You, we may, with Your help attain eternal rewards.
$Per Dominum

[Commemoratio Oratio]
!For St. Theodore, Martyr
O God, Who surround and protect us with the glorious profession of blessed~
Theodore, Your Martyr, grant us to profit by imitating him and to rejoice in his~
intercession.
$Per Dominum

[Commemoratio Secreta]
!For St. Theodore, Martyr
Accept, O Lord, the prayers and sacrificial gifts of Your faithful people, that,~
through these rites of love and devotion we may pass over to the glory of~
heaven.
$Per Dominum

[Commemoratio Postcommunio]
!For St. Theodore, Martyr
Grant, we beseech You, O Lord, by the intercession of blessed Theodore, Your~
Martyr, that what we taste with our lips we may consume with a pure heart.
$Per Dominum
