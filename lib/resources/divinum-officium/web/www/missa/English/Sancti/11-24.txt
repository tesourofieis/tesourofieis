Gloria

[Oratio]
O God, Who made blessed John, Your Confessor and Teacher, an extraordinary lover~
of perfect self-denial and the Cross, grant that, by continually striving to~
imitate him, we may reach everlasting glory.
$Per Dominum

[Secreta]
May the loving prayer of blessed John, Your Confessor and Doctor, fail us never, O~
Lord; may it commend our offerings and ever secure for us Your forgiveness.
$Per eumdem

[Postcommunio]
So that your sacrificial rites may grant us salvation, we pray You, O Lord, that~
blessed John, Your Confessor and illustrious Doctor, may draw nigh as our~
intercessor.
$Per Dominum

[Commemoratio Oratio]
!For St. Chrysogonus
Harken, O Lord, to our humble prayers, so that we who realize our guilt because~
of the evil we have committed, may be spared through the intercession of blessed~
Chrysogonus.
$Per Dominum

[Commemoratio Secreta]
!For St. Chrysogonus
Be appeased by the gifts we offer You, O Lord, and through the intercession of~
blessed Chrysogonus, Your Martyr, safeguard us from all dangers.
$Per Dominum

[Commemoratio Postcommunio]
!For St. Chrysogonus
Through the reception of Your sacrament, O Lord, may we be cleansed from our~
hidden sins and delivered from the snares of our enemies.
$Per Dominum
