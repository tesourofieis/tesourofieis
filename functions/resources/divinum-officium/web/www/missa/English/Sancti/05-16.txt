[Oratio]
Mercifully give us Your help, we beseech You, O Lord, and by the intercession of~
blessed Ubaldus, Your Confessor and Bishop, stretch over us the right hand of~
Your mercy against all wickedness of the devil.

[Secreta]
May Your Saints, we beseech You, O Lord, everywhere make us joyful: so that,~
while we reflect upon their merits, we may enjoy their help.
$Per Dominum

[Postcommunio]

received, we may, by the intercession of blessed Ubaldus, Your Confessor and~
Bishop, obtain still greater blessings.
