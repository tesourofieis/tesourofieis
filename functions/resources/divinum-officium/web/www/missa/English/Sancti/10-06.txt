[Oratio]
May we be aided by the intercession of St. Bruno, Your Confessor, we beseech You, O
Lord; that we, who have grievously offended Your Majesty by sin, may, by his merits and prayers, obtain forgiveness for our offenses.
$Per Dominum

[Secreta]
We offer You sacrifices of praise, O Lord, in memory of Your Saints; trusting~
that by them we may be delivered from both present and future evils.
$Per Dominum

[Postcommunio]
We pray You, almighty God, that we who have received heavenly nourishment, may~
thereby, through the intercession of blessed Bruno, Your Confessor, be sustained~
against all harm and danger.
$Per Dominum
