[Oratio]
Almighty God, Giver of all good things, Who willed that blessed Rose, imbued in~
early life with the dew of heavenly grace, should bloom among the Indians with~
the beauty of virginity and patience, grant unto us, Your servants, that,~
following the fragrance of her sweetness, we may be found worthy to become a~
sweet savor of Christ.
$Qui vivis


[Secreta]
May the offering made by Your devoted people be pleasing to You, O Lord, in~
honor of Your Saints, through whose merits they know they have received help in~
time of trial.
$Per Dominum

[Postcommunio]
You have filled Your people, O Lord, with sacred gifts; ever comfort us by the~
intercession of her whose festival we are celebrating.
$Per Dominum

[Commemoratio Oratio]
!For Sts. Felix and Adauctus
We humbly entreat Your Majesty, O Lord, that as You continually gladden us by~
the commemoration of Your Saints, so may You ever protect us as a result of~
their prayers.
$Per Dominum

[Commemoratio Secreta]
!For Sts. Felix and Adauctus
Look with favor, O Lord, on the offerings of Your people; may they find~
profitable for their salvation that rite which they devoutly perform in honor of~
Your Saints.
$Per Dominum

[Commemoratio Postcommunio]
!For Sts. Felix and Adauctus
Filled by the holy sacrament, O Lord, we beseech You that, by the intercession~
of Your Saints, we may spend our lives in giving thanks to You.
$Per Dominum
