[Oratio]
O God, Who prepared a pleasing dwelling place for Yourself in the heart of~
blessed Gertrude the Virgin, by her merits and intercession, mercifully wipe~
away all sinful stain from our heart and grant that we may enjoy her~
companionship.

[Secreta]
May the offering made by Your devoted people be pleasing to You, O Lord, in~
honor of Your Saints, through whose merits they know they have received help in~
times of trial.
$Per Dominum

[Postcommunio]

comfort us by the intercession of her whose festival we are celebrating.
