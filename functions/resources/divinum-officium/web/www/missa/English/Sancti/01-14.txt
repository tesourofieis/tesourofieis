[Oratio]
O God, Who gave to Your people blessed Hilary, as a minister of salvation,~
grant, we beseech You, that we who cherished him on earth as a teacher of life,~
may be found worthy to have him as an intercessor in heaven.
$Per Dominum

[Commemoratio Oratio]
!Commemoration of St. Felix, Priest and Martyr
Grant, we beseech You, almighty God, that the example of Your saints may urge us~
on to a better life, so that we may imitate the deeds of those whose feasts we~
celebrate.
$Per Dominum

[Commemoratio Secreta]
!Commemoration of St. Felix, Priest and Martyr
Graciously accept, O Lord, the sacrifices we offer You in honor of Your blessed~
Martyr Felix, and grant that they may bring us everlasting help.
$Per Dominum

[Commemoratio Postcommunio]
!Commemoration of St. Felix, Priest and Martyr
Refreshed by the sacrament of salvation, we beseech You, O Lord, that we may be~
helped by the prayers of blessed Felix, Your Martyr, whose feast we are~
celebrating.
$Per Dominum
