Gloria

[Oratio]
O God, Who gladden us with the yearly festival of blessed Blaise, Your Martyr and Bishop, mercifully grant that, as we venerate the anniversary of his martyrdom, we may also rejoice in his protection.
$Per Dominum

[Secreta]
Sanctify the offerings dedicated to You, O Lord, and through the intercession of~
blessed Blaise, Your Martyr and Bishop, look upon us with mercy because of them.
$Per Dominum

[Postcommunio]
May this Communion, O Lord, cleanse us of sin, and through the intercession of~
blessed Blaise, Your Martyr and Bishop, may it make us sharers of heavenly healing.
$Per Dominum
