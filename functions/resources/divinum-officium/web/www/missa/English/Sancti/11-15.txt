[Oratio]
O God, Who made blessed Albert, Your Bishop and Doctor, eminent in the~
submission of human wisdom to divine faith, grant us, we beseech You, so to~
follow the path of his teaching that we may enjoy perfect light in heaven.
$Per Dominum

[Secreta]
Look favorably upon these sacrificial gifts, O Lord, we beseech You, so that~
what we perform in the sacrificial rite of the passion of Your Son, our Lord, we~
may receive with grateful affection by the intercession and example of blessed~
Albert.
$Per eumdem

[Postcommunio]
Through this holy food which we have eaten, defend us, O Lord, from the~
onslaughts of our enemies and grant that by the intercession of blessed Albert,~
Your Confessor and Bishop, we may always live in peace.
$Per Dominum
