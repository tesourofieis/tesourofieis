[Oratio]
O God, lover of chastity, Who endowed with heavenly gifts blessed Mary Magdalen, a~
virgin on fire with love for You, grant that we who keep this feast-day in her~
honor may imitate her by purity and love.
$Per Dominum

[Secreta]
May the offering made by Your devoted people be pleasing to You, O Lord, in~
honor of Your Saints, through whose merits they know they have received help in~
times of trial.
$Per Dominum

[Postcommunio]
You have filled Your people, O Lord, with sacred gifts; we beseech You to ever~
comfort us by the intercession of her whose festival we are celebrating.
$Per Dominum
